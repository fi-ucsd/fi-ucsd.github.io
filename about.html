A new taste in UX

We're a user experience (UX) agency at UC San Diego.

We work to promote partnerships in design between the student and the industry .

We empower design collaboration

When we started Fi, we wanted to find a way to connect aspiring design students to projects that would help them learn and grow their skills in design work.

Now, we have a team of researchers and designers who are prepared to deliver to clients of all sizes, with all challenges.

We work beyond digital products

One of our biggest goals is working outside of the typical UX domain of digital products. We want to help you focus on how to improve customer experience for services, physical spaces, and special events.

If you're interested in being a client, but are still unsure if you fit in to our line of work, please don't hesitate to get in touch.
